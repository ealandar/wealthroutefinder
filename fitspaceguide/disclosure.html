Transparency about how Fit Space Guide earns revenue

FTC Disclosure

In accordance with the Federal Trade Commission's guidelines, Fit Space Guide would like to disclose that some of the links on this website are affiliate links. This means that if you click on the link and purchase an item, we may receive an affiliate commission at no extra cost to you.

Amazon Affiliate ID: As an Amazon Associate, we earn from qualifying purchases. Our Amazon affiliate tag is ivanykallc-20. Clicking our links and making a purchase supports our site at no additional cost to you.

How Affiliate Links Work

When you click on an affiliate link on our website and make a purchase, the retailer recognizes that you were referred by us. If you complete a qualifying purchase, we receive a small percentage of the sale as a commission. The price you pay remains the same whether you use our affiliate link or go directly to the retailer's website.

Our Commitment to Honesty

We want to emphasize the following principles that guide our content:

  • Editorial Independence: Our affiliate relationships do not influence our product reviews, ratings, or recommendations. We recommend products based on their quality, value, and suitability for home fitness use.
  • Honest Reviews: We provide balanced reviews that include both advantages and disadvantages of every product. If a product has significant drawbacks, we will tell you.
  • No Pay-for-Play: Companies cannot pay us to write favorable reviews. Our opinions are our own and are based on research and analysis.
  • Best Interest: Our primary goal is to help you make informed purchasing decisions. We would rather lose a commission by being honest than earn one by misleading our readers.

Other Affiliate Programs

In addition to Amazon Associates, we may participate in other affiliate programs in the future. Any additional affiliate relationships will be disclosed on this page. All affiliate partnerships are subject to the same editorial integrity standards described above.

Advertising

We may display advertisements on our website in the future. These advertisements may use cookies and other tracking technologies. Any advertising relationships will be disclosed and will not influence our editorial content.

Product Reviews and Recommendations

All product reviews and recommendations on Fit Space Guide are based on:

  • Thorough research of product specifications and features
  • Analysis of verified customer reviews and feedback
